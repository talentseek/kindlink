Corporate Fund & Grant Management

We provide companies with the ability to manage their Charitable Fund or Foundation grant process in a simple and cost-effective way. Working with the KindLink Foundation (a registered charitable organisation) allows us to reduce the administrative workload and cost of managing community funds for our corporate partners. As part of this service, we have implemented automated and manual security checks in order to vet the charities that receive the funds. This includes a Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)-regulated process.
